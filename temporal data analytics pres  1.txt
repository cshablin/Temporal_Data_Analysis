Course overview:

The main goal is the project.
- SIG-KDD style 6-8 pages paper report
- Num studs : 2 (1,3 optional)
- Topics : application of TDA method on a temporal dataset/s
- Students who have a temporal problem in their thesis are encourage to work on them
  after approval
  Otherwise, the students will implement a published paper -after approval
- There will be a quiz on the course materials.


Regarding the academic paper:
Format:
- Abstract
- Introduction
- Background
- Methods
- Evaluation (research questions, data, evaluation plan)
- Results
- Discussion


Analytics of data in time - topisc list:
-Time point series univariate
- MTD - 
- Forecasting
- Clustering
- Classification
- Patterns Discovery
+MORE.

GOAL : PROJECT 
Outcomes : 
- Intermediate deliverables : 
-- project proposal in one page (Introduction (motivation), methods, data, evaluation goals)
-- Lit survey of two pages (with at least 10 refs)
-- presentation of 10 min (not more than 7 slides)
   = 3-4 lit survey + 2 project methods + 1 Data + 1 Experimental Plan

-Final Deliverables:
-- Project report of 6-8 pages in SIG-KDD format
 = download the format from ACM SIGKDD 2018 CFP
-- Corresponding presentation of 15 minutes
